## Telangana Elections Dashboard — MLA & MP Results 2023
A Power BI dashboard built during my internship at Hareesh IT Industries, visualising the 2023 Telangana State Assembly (MLA) and Parliamentary (MP) election results across all constituencies.

## What it does
The dashboard lets you explore election results at the constituency, district, and state level — winner, winning party, vote share, margin of victory, and voter turnout — for both the MLA and MP elections held in Telangana in 2023.
This was one of the first projects where I saw a stakeholder actually change their understanding of a political outcome based on something I built. That's what made it stick.

** What the data covers
## MLA Elections (State Assembly)

All 119 Telangana constituencies
Winner, winning party, votes received, voting %
Runner-up party, runner-up votes, margin of victory
Districts covered: Hyderabad, Ranga Reddy, Medchal-Malkajgiri, Karimnagar, Nizamabad, Nalgonda, Warangal, and more

## MP Elections (Parliamentary)

Lok Sabha constituency results across Telangana
Same structure: winner, party, votes, margin

Parties covered: Indian National Congress (INC), Bharat Rashtra Samithi (BRS), Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), AIMIM, and others

## Key findings

INC swept the 2023 MLA elections — winning the majority of seats after BRS had dominated Telangana politics for a decade
BRS suffered significant losses despite high vote shares in several constituencies — the vote-to-seat conversion was poor
AIMIM held its stronghold in Hyderabad's Old City constituencies (Charminar, Chandrayangutta, Nampally, Karwan, Bahadurpura, Yakutpura) with commanding margins
Closest contest: Chevella constituency — INC won by just 268 votes
Largest margin: Chandrayangutta — Akbaruddin Owaisi (AIMIM) won by 81,660 votes with 64.89% voter turnout
Highest turnout constituency: Nakrekal — 60.97% voting percentage, INC won by 68,839 votes
